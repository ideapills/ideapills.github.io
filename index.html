『闪念胶囊』(Idea Pills)
是锤子科技在 2017 年 5 月 9 日发布手机中的一项新功能。
使用语音快速输入信息并分类记录成『胶囊』。
可解决灵感一瞬即逝的问题，并快速处理繁杂的日常待办事项。
胶囊可按照分类快速整合排序，也可被拖拽进文档快速编辑。

···

Idea Pills
was a new feature of Hammer Technology's mobile phone released on May 9, 2017.
It uses voice to quickly capture information and save it into "capsules".
It solves the problem of inspiration fleeting, and quickly deal with complex daily affairs.

···

I like this idea of capturing fleeting ideas and daily affairs.
And I always use Eisenhower Decision Matrix to categorize affairs by their importance and urgency.
But no similar App is found. So I did a web app for my own usage.
If you are interested also welcome to use: ideapills.xyz/app.
